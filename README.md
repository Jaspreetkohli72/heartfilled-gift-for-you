# Heartfilled Gift For You

A cozy, low-stimulation digital care package designed for Komal to rest, relax, and heal.

## What is Inside
*   **A Little Warmth**: A text comfort engine with comforting and personal notes.
*   **Comforting Sounds**: A custom audio player playing ambient rain sounds and soft lo-fi cozy music, initialized at a safe volume (35%).
*   **Comfort Stream**: A visual gallery of custom illustrations and happy memories from video calls.
